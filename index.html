You can publish a book! Right now!

Book publishing is radically different from what it was just a few years ago, and it is insanely easy to publish a buyable book with the right mentality and a few simple techniques & tools. This website is supporting an “intersession” at Girls’ Middle School, but the content may be useful on its own.

Goals

For you to have a printed book in your hands by the end of the week, and for you to have a buyable book on Amazon also by the end of the week. These are the core goals.

Additional goals would be for you to understand traditional publishing through “literary agents”, a bit about different book sizes, costs, pricing, translation, editing, and so on.

Relevant Bio

A year ago, I was not a mass-market published author. I had written things and printed things, and people had read and valued what I wrote. But I had never had my own book, buyable by anyone. But back in May, all that changed. I started writing a memoir The Slums of Palo Alto and within a week I had a book. And by the end of September, I had a buyable book. I may have been rejected by 42 literary agents (yes forty-two agents), but I was a published author. You can buy and read my book today if you want!

My goal for the intersession is to show you how easy publishing a book is. I am not going to teach you how to write. You must come with your own writing, or borrow someone else’s (maybe from http://www.gutenberg.org/). But I will teach you how to transform words on paper into a book. And transform a book into a buyable book. It is totally awesome! And you will be too! (Well, more awesome than you already are :–)
